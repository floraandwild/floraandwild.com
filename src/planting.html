Planting Design

Based in Linlithgow, Flora & WILD provides complete Planting Design services from initial survey to planting and ongoing care. Whether you're starting with a blank canvas or a mature garden that needs a fresh touch, we're here to help you create a beautiful, thriving space that feels uniquely yours.

Our Approach

Our approach is tailored to your tastes, your garden’s environment, and the vision you have in mind. Get excited to spend time in a beautiful outdoor space, where every season brings fresh blooms and nature thrives all around you.

Prices start from £250 depending on the size and scale of your project.

How We Deliver

Site Survey & Assessment

I begin with an in-depth assessment of your garden’s unique conditions, including soil type, sunlight, drainage, and microclimates, to ensure that every plant chosen will thrive in your space.

Personalised Planting Plan

Every garden is unique, and so is every planting plan. I’ll create a tailored design that reflects your preferences, from colour schemes and textures to plant types and arrangements. My designs are naturalistic, focusing on sustainable, resilient planting that supports local wildlife.

Plant Selection & Sourcing

I carefully select plants that will not only complement each other but also provide seasonal interest throughout the year. I can source high-quality, sustainable plants suited to your garden, ensuring longevity and resilience in your planting scheme.

Implementation & Planting

Once the design is ready, I can manage or assist with the planting process to bring the vision to life. With careful attention to placement and planting techniques, I ensure that each plant has the best start in its new environment.

Ongoing Support & Maintenance Advice

To help your garden continue to thrive, I provide guidance on plant care and maintenance. This can include advice on watering, feeding, and seasonal tasks, so your garden looks beautiful year-round and becomes a source of enjoyment for years to come.
